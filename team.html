Meet Our Team

Say hello to Kaitlin, the developer and creative force behind our team! Kaitlin brings a unique blend of artistic flair and strategic thinking to our projects. Her imaginative approach to challenges and keen eye for design make her an essential part of our creative journey. Outside of work, Kaitlin enjoys exploring art exhibits, discovering new music, and finding inspiration in the world around her.







Kaitlin Penaranda

Kaitlin's journey began with a passion for drawing, where she used a pencil to weave imaginative stories on paper. Transitioning to the violin, she explored the world of music, creating melodies that spoke volumes without words. Alongside her artistic pursuits, Kaitlin embraced the discipline of karate, cultivating strength and focus.



In college, Kaitlin unexpectedly ventured into coding, discovering a new canvas for her creativity. Kaitlin's story is a testament to the diverse paths one can explore in a lifetime. From the strokes of a pencil to the harmonious tunes of a violin, and the disciplined moves of karate, she has woven a rich tapestry of experiences. Coding, the latest addition to her repertoire, is yet another medium through which Kaitlin continues to shape her narrative, showcasing the endless possibilities that a journey through art, music, and discipline can unfold.
